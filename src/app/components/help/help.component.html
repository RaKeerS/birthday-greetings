• The left panel titled 'Greeting Template Details' contains information fields to be filled.
  • Entering details in the left panel will update and display the same on the right panel containing the greeting template view.
  • Some fields are marked as required hence, these fields needs to be filled before trying to 'Save' the template or 'Submit' the template as email.
  • While entering email related fields, hit 'Enter' button to add new email id.
  • The last email field titled Sender's Email takes only one email which is doesn't require hitting the 'Enter' after completion.
  • When the toggle switch under 'Recipient Info.' and 'Sender Info.' is switched to be off/disabled, it would remove certain aspects of the template.
    This allows user to customize the template message as per their need.
    When disabled certain elements of the template won't appear in the downloaded image or sent email via. clicking on the buttons at the bottom of the 'Greeting Template Details' form.
